ABOUT.

This app equips us with tools to calculate the day we were born.

With just the details of your birth,that is date,month and year,it gives us our original birthday.

It also shows us what our Akan names are.

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.

GHANIAN NAMES.

In Ghana, it is a culture to them to name new born babies using day names as their frist names and according to gender too.

For example,if born on Sunday and a male,Kwasi would be your first name.

Also,there a specific names given to twins,although this website will not look into that for now.
